How to manage your salary

You can manage your salary by using this app

Enter your salary:

  • 50% for needs: Allocate half of your income to cover essential expenses such as housing, utilities, groceries, transportation, healthcare, and minimum debt payments.
  • 30% for wants: Reserve 30% of your income for discretionary spending on non-essential items like dining out, entertainment, vacations, hobbies, and personal preferences.
  • 20% for savings and debt repayment: Dedicate 20% of your income towards building savings, creating an emergency fund, contributing to retirement, and paying down debts to secure your financial future.
